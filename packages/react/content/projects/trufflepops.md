A comprehensive mental health and wellness platform designed to help users understand their emotions, track their thoughts, and grow personally. TrufflePops provides a secure, private space for emotional well-being with:

- **Mood Tracking**: Monitor emotional patterns and identify triggers with detailed analytics
- **Daily Journaling**: Record thoughts, feelings, and experiences in a private, secure space
- **Progress Timeline**: View growth over time with interactive visualizations of personal journey
- **Privacy Focused**: End-to-end encryption ensures user data stays private and secure
- **Responsive Design**: Accessible across all devices for consistent user experience
- **Data Visualization**: Interactive charts and graphs to understand emotional trends
- **Secure Architecture**: Built with privacy-first principles and robust security measures

This platform demonstrates expertise in creating sensitive, user-focused applications that prioritize mental health and data privacy while providing meaningful insights for personal growth.
